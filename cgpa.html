How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select your engineering branch from the options
  2. Choose your current semester
  3. Enter grades for all your subjects
  4. Your GPA will be calculated automatically
  5. CGPA will update as you add more semesters

Grading Scale

  • O (Outstanding) = 10 points
  • A+ (Excellent) = 9 points
  • A (Very Good) = 8 points
  • B+ (Good) = 7 points
  • B (Average) = 6 points
  • C (Pass) = 5 points
  • U (Fail) = 0 points

Features

  • Performance insights and analytics
  • Grade distribution visualization
  • Export results as image
  • Share results with others
  • Predictive GPA analysis

    About IB studioz GPA Calculator

    IB studioz GPA Calculator is a comprehensive tool designed for engineering students to accurately calculate their GPA and CGPA. Our mission is to provide students with an intuitive, powerful, and visually appealing platform to track their academic performance.

    Key Features

    • Accurate Calculations: Precise GPA and CGPA calculations based on official grading scales
    • Visual Analytics: Interactive charts and graphs to visualize your academic progress
    • Performance Insights: Intelligent analysis of your academic performance with recommendations
    • Predictive Analytics: Future GPA predictions based on your current performance trends
    • Export & Share: Easily export your results as images or share them with others
    • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile

    How It Works

    Simply select your engineering branch and semester, then input your grades for each subject. The calculator will automatically compute your GPA for that semester and update your overall CGPA. As you add more semesters, you'll see insights and patterns in your academic performance.

    At IB studioz, we're committed to providing educational tools that empower students to take control of their academic journey. We continuously improve our calculator based on user feedback and the latest educational standards.
